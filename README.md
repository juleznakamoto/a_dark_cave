# A Dark Cave

**Survive the Darkness, Build Your Settlement**

A text-based survival and settlement building game. Start with nothing in the depths of an ancient cave and build your way to survival. Gather resources, craft tools, manage villagers, and uncover dark secrets.

## Play Now

**[Play A Dark Cave](https://a-dark-cave.com)**

## Features

- **Text-Based Gameplay** - Immersive narrative-driven experience with minimalist interface
- **Incremental Progression** - Start from nothing and gradually unlock new mechanics and content
- **Resource Management** - Gather wood, food, and materials to survive
- **Settlement Building** - Construct buildings and expand your village
- **Villager Management** - Recruit and assign workers to different tasks
- **World Exploration** - Venture beyond the cave to discover new locations and secrets
- **Persistent Progress** - Your game saves automatically so you can continue anytime
- **Mobile Friendly** - Play on any device with a modern browser

## Gameplay

Begin by lighting a fire in a dark cave. As you progress, you'll unlock new abilities:

1. **The Cave** - Tend the fire and craft basic tools
2. **The Village** - Build structures and manage villagers
3. **The World** - Explore the wilderness and uncover mysteries

## Technology

Built with React, TypeScript, and modern web technologies for a smooth browser-based gaming experience.
