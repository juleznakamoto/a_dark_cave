A Dark Cave is a text based survival game

A Dark Cave is a minimalist text based survival and settlement building game playable directly in your browser. Start with nothing, gather resources, manage villagers and uncover dark secrets in the depths of an ancient cave.

An incremental RPG inspired by A Dark Room, Darkest Dungeon, The Ensign, Candy Box, Kittens Game, Eldrum and Lovecraftian horror.
