A Dark Cave – Text-Based Survival & Incremental Browser Game

A Dark Cave is a free text-based survival and settlement building game playable in your browser. Explore an ancient cave, find the remains of a lost civilization, and uncover their dark secrets. Gather resources, build a settlement, and manage villagers as you push deeper into a world that doesn't want to be found. No download required. The game combines incremental idle mechanics with narrative-driven gameplay, inspired by classics like A Dark Room, Candy Box, Kittens Game, and Darkest Dungeon. Start by lighting a fire in the darkness and gradually unlock new abilities: tend the cave, craft tools, construct buildings, recruit villagers, and venture into the wilderness. Each playthrough offers different choices and consequences, with turn-based combat, crafting systems, and companions who bring their own storylines. Progress saves automatically so you can continue anytime. The game runs on desktop and mobile browsers with no installation needed.

An Incremental Survival RPG

Inspired by A Dark Room, Candy Box, Kittens Game, and Darkest Dungeon. Incremental idle mechanics meet settlement building, resource management, turn-based combat, and Lovecraftian horror.

Gather, Craft, Upgrade

Collect resources, craft tools and weapons, upgrade buildings and skills through multiple tiers. Assign villagers to jobs, unlock new schematics, and expand your settlement to survive what lies ahead. A Dark Cave features a deep progression system with wood, food, stone, and rare materials gathered from the cave and surrounding areas. Craft tools like axes and picks, then weapons and armor for turn-based combat. Build structures including the workshop, barracks, and shrine, each unlocking new capabilities. Villagers can be assigned to gathering, crafting, or construction tasks. Schematics unlock advanced items like the arbalest, nightshade bow, and stormglass halberd. The settlement grows from a single fire to a fortified village as you push deeper into the world. Idle mode lets your settlement produce resources while you're away, and the game saves progress automatically in your browser for both desktop and mobile play.

Fellowship Companions

Recruit companions with their own storylines. A wandering knight, an aging wizard, a wildling huntress bound by blood oath, and many more. Each is earned through choices and changes how you play.

Explore a Dark World

Venture into ancient forests, forgotten ruins, and hostile lands. Encounter strangers, face attack waves, and confront things that should have stayed buried.

Combat & Crafting

Fight enemies in turn-based combat with crafted weapons, bombs, and combat skills. Build defenses to protect your settlement from what comes out of the dark.

Dark Fantasy Atmosphere

The story unfolds through events, choices, and discovery. Build shrines and monuments. Unlock forbidden knowledge. Some choices strengthen your people. Others lead to madness.

Play Free in Your Browser

Free HTML5 browser game on desktop and mobile. Progress saves locally. Idle mode lets your settlement grow while you are away.
